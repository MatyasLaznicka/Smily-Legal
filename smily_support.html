Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take a teeth scan?

Tap the Scan tab, allow camera access, and follow the on-screen instructions to capture a clear photo of your teeth.

How do I access premium features?

Tap the subscription offer after your first scan, or go to Profile → Manage Subscription to upgrade.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Profile → Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored if you previously purchased it with this Apple ID.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All photos and personal data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.
